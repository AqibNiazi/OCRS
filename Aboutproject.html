About project

The purpose of this project is to automate the existing system with new web-based Application where people can report crime online. It provides the facility of uploading images or videos of crime scenes so that police can take immediate action. Users of these systems can report any emergency situation. With this new system valuable data can be stored for a long period of time with easy data accessing and manipulation.
It also provides missing persons details, most wanted criminals of particular city, statistics of different crime and security tips for the awareness of people.
Presently there is no online web application available to report crime online. In order to report any complains associated with crime, people have to contact closest police station. People of the specific city are not aware of crime related matters such as list of Most wanted criminals of their town, newest crime related news, missing persons of the area etc. People must view News Channels or Read News Paper for such offense related details. Thus, we can say that present platform is manual and does not supply all the information from one source. In short with this system police depart will manage the details of criminals, crime, Case’s, Reports and FIRs in effective manner. The system will be built by keeping in view users, Employees and Administrator of the system.
User: Registered users report any criminal activity they face ore near to them. They can also case FIR online with this system.
Employees: Employees will add new cases after authentication, add criminal records, update criminals and case reports, view emergency complaints.
Administrator: Admin add and remove new users and Employees, update and delete complaints, update, and close any Case, View/Delete Emergency Complaint, View Query, View Feedback. This software helps police department to maintain record without redundant entries so that relevant information can be obtained easily and effectively.
